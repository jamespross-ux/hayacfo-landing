GET STARTED
1
Add it to your home screen
iPHONE In Safari, tap the Share icon, then Add to Home Screen.
ANDROID In Chrome, tap the menu, then Add to Home Screen or Install app.
It opens full-screen like a native app — no browser needed each time.
2
Add your accounts & investments
Go to the Update tab to enter your balances and portfolio values. Add as many or as few as you like — every time you update, HayaCFO saves a snapshot so your net worth over time builds up automatically.
3
Set your display currency
In Setup, under Display Currency, choose your preferred headline currency (GBP, AED, USD, INR…). Your data stays in its original currency — this just changes how it's shown to you.
THEN TRY THIS
Once your numbers are in, open the CFO Chat tab and hit "Start interview (10 questions)". HayaCFO asks a set of questions tailored to your actual accounts, goals and cash flow — the fastest way to get a sharp, personalised read. After that, ask it anything you like.
